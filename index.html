“If someone hates you so much that they try to hurt you,
defending yourself is not being aggressive, it is doing what’s necessary”
Charles F. Glassman, Brain Drain The Breakthrough That Will Change Your Life
We teach real life self defence. In other words we teach self defence based on the human instinctive reactions to stress or danger. We teach basic skills and techniques that you can use in case of an attack and more importantly that you will remember! Our instructors have all trained in various martial arts. These are wonderful skills to have and such excellent sports to participate in but you need to train regularly to have any kind of proficiency in them. Our seminars give you basic, easy to remember techniques based on everyone’s natural human reaction to finding themselves in a dangerous and stressful situation. Anyone can learn these skills regardless of their previous experience level. Our students learn to adapt the strikes we teach them to the situation they may find themselves in and we drill them in realistic scenarios to ensure that these lessons stick.
