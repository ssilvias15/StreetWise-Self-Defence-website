What about us?

We teach self defence to anyone who wants to learn. Contact us - we will be happy to discuss it !

Fiona Flavin

Fiona has a black belt in kickboxing and self defence, she trained for three years in krav maga and has also trained in combatives and senshido. When drilling with her fellow instructor, Stephen, to teach classes, Fiona unashamedly admits to only using what works for her from all the different areas of training she has had. “ … if it doesn’t work for me as a 5,2” female against a taller stronger male, I tend not to teach it - particularly when teaching women or girls in our teen programmes. “ “...Self defence has to be based on what you are most likely to do when caught by surprise and are vulnerable”.

Stephen McDonald

Stephen has trained extensively in krav maga. He has also trained in senshido, combatives and jiu jitsu. Stephen has adapted his training in jiu jitsu to create some very effective ground techniques. “when an attack is taken to the ground it immediately becomes more sinister ….the attacker is looking for more than a wallet …... you have to cause maximum harm in minimum time to protect yourself”
