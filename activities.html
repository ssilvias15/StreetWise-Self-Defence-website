Teen Program

We are extremely proud of our teen program! We teach in secondary schools throughout Dublin, usually in Transition Year and have had fantastic results. We are consistently amazed by the understanding and enthusiasm and never ending questions and "what ifs" from these fantastic people. No two classes are ever the same and we revel in the challenge of their unceasing curiosity while at the same time trying to figure out their place in the world. We also do teen seminars.

Ladies only

The fact is women are usually attacked by men. Unfortunately a lot of women who approach us looking for training have already experienced some kind of assault and want to be better prepared should this happen again or continue to happen. We are so glad to be able to help them with this.
